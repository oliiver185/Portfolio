Since coming to the UK, I wanted to deepen my fundamental understanding of how websites, apps and other programmes are built and function, and so I studied many coding languages on the Web Developer Course with JustIT.


I am a fast learner and constantly working on a new project, whether that’s an app to help children spell, creating recipe databases with APIs, hand-coding a website for a family member and I am discovering more and more that the sky is the limit with coding! I can quickly identify problems and work out how to fix them, as well as being creative with web design and cater to the client’s needs and expectations. I am a confident, independent learner and constantly researching a new language, function or stylistic element to make my work even better. I am very detail focused; I want all the projects I work on to look perfect as well as function flawlessly, to be executed on time and on budget. I am excited to expand my knowledge further by working as a front-end developer in the IT industry. I am based in West London and very happy to travel into the office or work remotely as needed.
